Code for Boston

A Code for America brigade and grassroots organization.


We have resumed in-person hack nights for those who are able.

New members can join us online Tuesday nights at 7 p.m.! Existing members can also attend in-person at CIC (4th Floor Kitchen) by talking to their project lead. Here is how to get started:

  • Join our Slack team
  • Download Zoom
  • Join us for announcements and new member orientation
RSVP on Zoom Say hi on Slack See our work

Code for Boston addresses local social and civic challenges through creative uses of technology. Despite the name, we're not solely focused on coding! We foster relationships between government, nonprofit, academic, for-profit companies, residents, civic technologists, analysts, designers, and many more. All are welcome!
