We are temporarily suspending in-person hack nights

In light of the ongoing concerns around COVID-19—and based on guidance from Code for America and our hosts at the Cambridge Innovation Center— we have made the difficult decision to shift to virtual hack nights starting Tuesday, March 10. Please check on Slack or Meetup for further details.

This is a temporary phase! With your help, we’ll keep our community strong and stay on mission to improve the lives of everyone in the Greater Boston area through design and technology.

Code for Boston addresses local social and civic challenges through creative uses of technology. Despite the name, we're not solely focused on coding! We foster relationships between government, nonprofit, academic, for-profit companies, residents, civic technologists, analysts, designers, and many more. All are welcome!
